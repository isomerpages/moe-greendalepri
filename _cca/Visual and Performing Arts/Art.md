<h2><strong>Art</strong>&nbsp;</h2>
<p>Art Club is a safe space for the exploration and experiential learning
of Art beyond the boundaries of the classroom. It is also a platform for
students to extend their interest in Art. Opportunities are given for students
to participate in nationwide competition such as Singapore Youth Festival
(SYF), to go for learning journeys to the museum and to learn and create
art based on art techniques learnt.</p>

<br><strong>Title of artwork:</strong>&nbsp;Less is More
<br><strong>Medium:</strong>&nbsp;Mixed Media
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Obtained the Certificate of Recognition</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

</div>
<p><strong><u>Artist Statement</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The idea of staple food is used to symbolise technology which has become
an important element in our lives.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The healthy diet plate contrasts with the large quantity of food and technological
parts. Just like food, when digital consumption goes beyond its recommended
portion, it is not beneficial to us.&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong><u>Artist statement</u></strong>
</p>
<p>We created a neighbourhood precinct using a QR code as inspiration because
it has become an integral part of our lives. The QR code reads ‘LIFE’ from
the top.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The surroundings and the windows at the side of the buildings show scenes
of daily life.</p>
