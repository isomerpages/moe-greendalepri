<p><strong>Welcome to the Greendale PSG!</strong>
</p>
<p>The Greendale Primary School Parent Support Group (PSG) is a warm and
inclusive community of parents working in partnership with the school to
support our children’s learning, well-being and school life. Together,
we strengthen the home–school partnership with care, collaboration and
purpose.</p>
