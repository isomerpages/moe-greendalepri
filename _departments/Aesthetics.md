<h2><strong>Aesthetics</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>
</p>
<p>Every Greendalite is a reflective, imaginative, and engaged learner through
a rich arts education.</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
</p>
<p>To nurture every Greendalite's appreciation of music and art through creative,
experiential learning in local and global contexts.</p>
<p>At the heart of our Aesthetics Department lies a belief that learning
is most meaningful when it is lived. Through experiential learning, we
invite students to engage deeply with Art and Music — not merely as academic
subjects, but as powerful languages for self-expression, reflection, and
connection. Through experiential learning, we intentionally cultivate Emerging
21st Century Competencies (e21CC) such as adaptive, critical and inventive
thinking, communication and collaboration skills, equipping students with
the dispositions they need to thrive beyond the classroom.</p>
<hr>
<p><strong>Art: Seeing, Expressing, and Appreciating</strong>
</p>
<p>In our Art lessons, we aim to develop students who are confident in seeing,
expressing, and appreciating artworks. Students are guided to develop their
creative voice through Inquiry-Based Learning and Visible Thinking strategies
that encourage curiosity, exploration, and deeper reflection.</p>
<p>• <strong>Seeing</strong>: We teach students to observe the world with
a reflective eye, understanding the nuances of visual culture in various
contexts.</p>
<p>• <strong>Expressing</strong>: By nurturing imaginative thinking, we empower
students to find their unique voice and express themselves creatively.</p>
<p>• <strong>Appreciating</strong>: Through the study of art in local and
global contexts, Greendalites gain a broader awareness of the cultural
significance behind every masterpiece. They also learn to appreciate the
artworks and intentions of their peers.</p>

<p><strong>Music: Listening, Creating, and Performing</strong>
</p>
<p>Our Music department focuses on developing students who are confident
in listening, creating, and performing with musical instruments.</p>
<p>• <strong>Listening</strong>: We cultivate engaged learners who can listen
critically and appreciate the diversity of musical traditions from around
the world.</p>
<p>• <strong>Creating</strong>: Students are encouraged to be imaginative,
using their foundational knowledge to compose and create original musical
works.</p>
<p>• <strong>Performing</strong>: Through experiential learning, students
gain the confidence to perform on various instruments, translating their
creative expression into live musical experiences.</p>
