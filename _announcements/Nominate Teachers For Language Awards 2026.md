<p>Dear Parent/Guardian,</p>
<p>We would like to invite you to nominate deserving teachers for the following
teaching awards in the table below. These awards recognise educators who
demonstrate passion, excellence and impact in language teaching and learning.</p>
<p>Please take some time to consider teachers who have made meaningful contributions
in English, Tamil, Malay or Chinese Language education, and submit your
nominations accordingly.</p>
<p>Thank you for helping us celebrate the good work of our language teachers.</p>
