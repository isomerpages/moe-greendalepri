<p>The time has come to celebrate the outstanding educators who inspire,
guide and make significant contributions to the teaching and learning of
Malay Language and Tamil Language in our school.</p>
<p>Show your appreciation by nominating them for the <strong>Arif Budiman Malay Language Teacher Award (AGAB) 2025</strong> or
the <strong>Most Inspiring Tamil Teachers’ (MITT) Award 2025</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Nominations for both awards are now open.</strong>
</p>
<p>Please visit<strong> </strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/agab2025" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/agab2025</a><strong> </strong>(for
Arif Budiman Malay Language Teacher Award (AGAB) 2025) and<strong> </strong>&nbsp;
<a href="https://go.gov.sg/mitt2025" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/mitt2025</a><strong> </strong>(for Most Inspiring Tamil
Teachers’ Award 2025) to submit your nominations. <strong>Closing date for nominations is &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<u>4 April 2025</u>.</strong>
</p>
