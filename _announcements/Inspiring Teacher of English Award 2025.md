<p>Do you love your English teacher? Has your teacher inspired you to learn
and improve your use of English? Nominations for the annual Inspiring Teacher
of English Award are now open.</p>
<p>All students, their parents, and teachers are invited to send in nominations
for the Teaching Award. You can visit <a href="http://www.goodenglish.org.sg/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">http://www.goodenglish.org.sg/</a> to
download the nomination form for the Teaching Award. All completed nomination
forms should be submitted to the school by 6 p.m. on <strong><u>24 March 2025.</u></strong>
</p>
