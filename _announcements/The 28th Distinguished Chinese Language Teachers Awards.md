<p>The 28<sup>th</sup> Distinguished Chinese Language Teachers Awards is now
open for nominations.</p>
<p>This award aims to give due recognition to deserving Chinese language
teachers who have shown exemplary efforts towards the teaching of Chinese
language and culture in the past year. The award also affirms the efforts
of teachers who have successfully inculcated in their students an appreciation
for Chinese language and culture.</p>
<p>All students, their parents, and teachers are invited to send in online
nominations for the Distinguished Chinese Language Teachers Awards. Please
visit <a href="https://zb.sg/dclta2025" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">https://zb.sg/dclta2025</a> to
submit your nominations. <strong>Closing date for nominations is <u>11 May 2025</u>.</strong>
</p>
